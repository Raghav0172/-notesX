About NotesX

NotesX is a full-stack web application designed to help university students share and access academic notes efficiently.

Features

  • ✓ Upload Notes: Share your notes with the university community
  • ✓ Organize by Category: Department, year, subject, and topic
  • ✓ Search & Filter: Find notes quickly by any criteria
  • ✓ Download Tracking: See how many students have accessed your notes
  • ✓ Tagging System: Add custom tags for better organization

Technology Stack

Frontend: React.js, Vite, Material-UI

Backend: Node.js, Express.js

Database: MongoDB

Storage & Auth: Firebase
